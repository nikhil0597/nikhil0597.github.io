Research

My research interests include:

  • High-order numerical schemes for hyperbolic conservation laws
  • Structure preservation of numerical methods
  • Active flux method
  • Nonlocal conservation laws
  • Conservation laws with discontinuous flux
  • Rigorous convergence and stability analysis of numerical schemes
